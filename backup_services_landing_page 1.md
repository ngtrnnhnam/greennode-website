## 3. Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

**Q: What is the difference between Full and Incremental Backups?**  
A: A Full backup stores a complete copy of all your data. An Incremental backup only records changes made since the last session, significantly reducing storage costs and backup time while ensuring a full recovery path.

**Q: How does "Location Lock" protect my data?**  
A: Location Lock prevent backups from being deleted by any user for a specified duration. This provides an essential layer of security against accidental deletion and malicious ransomware attacks.

**Q: Can I customize my backup schedule?**  
A: Yes. You can create custom Backup Policies with frequencies ranging from hourly snapshots to monthly archives, ensuring your data protection aligns perfectly with your business needs.

**Q: What happens if I accidentally delete a backup?**  
A: Our **Soft Delete** feature ensures that deleted backups are held in a recovery state for a set number of days. You can restore them instantly if they were removed in error.

**Q: Is cross-region storage supported?**  
A: Yes. You can configure your Backup Location to store data in a different geographic region, providing ultimate disaster recovery protection against regional infrastructure failures.
