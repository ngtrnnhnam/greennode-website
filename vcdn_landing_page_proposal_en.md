## Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: How do I get started with GreenNode vCDN?**
A: You can set up your first CDN domain through the GreenNode Portal. Simply access the vCDN section, select your package, and follow the guided setup process.

**Q: Is there a free trial available?**
A: Yes! Each new GreenNode account includes **100GB of free CDN traffic** to help you test our performance. Trial accounts are limited to one CDN domain.

**Q: What happens if I use up my prepaid traffic?**
A: If your traffic volume is depleted, the service will be temporarily disabled. You can easily purchase "Extra Domestic" or "Extra International" traffic packages to resume service.

**Q: Does vCDN support SSL?**
A: Absolutely. You can use GreenNode’s default SSL certificate (*.vcdn.com.vn) or upload your own custom SSL certificate and private key for your specific domains.

**Q: How do I clear the cache if I update my content?**
A: You can use the "Cache Purge" feature in the portal to clear specific URLs or the entire domain. The number of allowed purges per day depends on your service package.

**Q: Can I delete a CDN domain?**
A: Currently, GreenNode supports editing or disabling CDN domains rather than full deletion to maintain service history and configuration integrity.
